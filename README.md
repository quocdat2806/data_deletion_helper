# Data Deletion Instructions

## English Pro Max - Data Deletion Policy

This application is designed for learning English and operates primarily on the user's device.

---

## 📌 What data do we store?

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

* Name
* Age
* Gender
* English proficiency level
* Learning goals

⚠️ This data is stored **only on your device** and is **not transmitted to any external server**, except for temporary audio processing via AI services.

---

## 🗑️ How to delete your data

Users can delete their data in the following ways:

### Method 1: Inside the app

1. Open the app
2. Go to **Profile**
3. Tap **"Reset Data"** or **"Delete Data"**

---

### Method 2: Uninstall the app

* Simply uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data from your device.

---

## 🔐 Data processing

* Audio recordings may be temporarily sent to third-party AI services (such as OpenAI) for speech-to-text processing.
* No personal data is stored or shared beyond this purpose.
